# Enraged Eggplant publication permission

The repository owner reports that Enraged Eggplant, author of the GURPS monster conversions in `Monsters (May 11, 2024)`, granted carte-blanche authorization to do "whatever I want without restriction" with that work. The project understands this to include permission to use, adapt, convert, publish, redistribute, and license the authorized work to downstream users.

In reliance on that authorization, reviewed EE-originated contributions and project adaptations included in a public release are offered under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International license. A standardized statement from Enraged Eggplant using the words "CC BY 4.0" has been requested to make the downstream grant easier to recognize, but the existing unrestricted authorization is the project's current permission basis.

Derived GCS templates and Dungeons On Automatic monster records must retain the following record-level provenance:

- `kind`: `fan_authorized`
- `sourceSystem`: `gurps_4e_fan_conversion`
- `sourceLicense`: `author_permission`
- `contentLicense`: `cc_by_4_0` for reviewed public releases
- author credit: `Enraged Eggplant`
- source title/version: `Monsters (May 11, 2024)`

Canonical public credit line:

> Original GURPS conversion by Enraged Eggplant, from *Monsters (May 11, 2024)*; adapted and republished with permission.

When EE-originated work survives in a derived artifact, this credit must be retained in structured package and record provenance, GCS references/notes, release notices, public-site details, and downstream displays or exports that present the credited statistics. See [`CREDITS.md`](../CREDITS.md) for the credit trigger, scope, and structured form.

This permission and CC BY 4.0 release apply only to rights held by Enraged Eggplant or otherwise authorized to the project. They do not grant rights in Wizards of the Coast or Steve Jackson Games text, artwork, trademarks, Product Identity, or other third-party expression.

This note records the authorization asserted by the repository owner on July 13, 2026 and clarified on July 14, 2026. Mechanical review status is independent of publication permission: automated conversions remain `review_required` until checked.
