# 3.5-era SRD OGL 1.0a Notes

3.5-era SRD conversion records use record-level provenance:

- `sourceSystem`: `srd_3_5`
- `sourceLicense`: `ogl_1_0a`
- `sourceUrl`: `https://www.d20srd.org/`
- `sourceCopyrightNotice`: the OGL Section 15 notice required for the converted source record.

Before publishing converted 3.5 records, verify the Open Game Content boundary, include the required OGL text/notice in release artifacts, and do not mix OGL records with CC-BY records without preserving `sourceLicense`.

Reference: https://www.d20srd.org/ogl.htm
